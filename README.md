📖 Overview
FaZe MENU is an advanced mod for the game Screw Drivers that adds over 50 physical modifications and vehicle control features. Developed as a client-side mod for MelonLoader, this menu provides complete control over your vehicle's physics, enabling everything from free flight to extreme gravity and speed manipulation.

🎮 Key Features
🚀 Flight and Movement Control
Free Flight (WASD): Control your car in the air using WASD, Space to ascend, and Shift to descend

Adjustable Flight Speed: Regulate flight speed from 10 to 300 units

Hover Mode: Keep your car suspended in the air without movement

Position Overwrite: Forces the car's position on the server every 0.03 seconds to prevent rubberbanding in multiplayer

🌍 Gravity Manipulation
Zero Gravity: Completely removes gravity

Super Gravity x4: Quadruples gravity for heavier driving experience

Custom Gravity: Adjust gravity between -3x and 6x

⚡ Engine Boosting
Super Acceleration (W): Exponential acceleration when holding W

Passive Turbo: Continuous turbo force with adjustable power

Acceleration & Turbo Force: Sliders to adjust power output

🛠️ Physical Control
Freeze Position: Stop the car completely in mid-air

Lock Rotation: Prevent the car from rotating

Kinematic Mode: Disable physics for complete control

Adjustable Mass: Change vehicle weight (1-8000 kg)

🎯 Assistance and Stability
Auto-Flip: Automatically right the car when upside down

Adjustable Downforce: Add aerodynamic load for better grip

Constant Speed: Maintain a fixed speed

Air Gyroscope: Rotation control while flying

🔄 Quick Actions and Stunts
Super Jump: Instant vertical impulse

Forward/Backward Dash: Quick impulses in the car's direction

Frontflip/Backflip/Rolls: Aerial stunts with one button

Flatspin: Rapid flat spins

Launch into Space: Extreme vertical impulse

Ground Slam: Rapid descent to the ground

📍 Teleportation and Waypoints
Save Point: Store your current position

Load Point: Teleport to the saved position

Go to Origin: Teleport to coordinates (0,10,0)

+5m Up / -3m Down: Quick vertical movement

📐 Vehicle Scaling
Adjustable X, Y, Z Scale: Change car size on each axis

🎮 Controls
INSERT: Open/Close the menu

P (or configured key): Toggle flight mode on/off

WASD: Movement during flight

Space: Ascend

Shift: Descend

🔧 Compatibility
Singleplayer: Works perfectly

Multiplayer: Not work yet

MelonLoader: Requires MelonLoader v0.6.0 or higher
